# RwandaFitness V2

> **The Home of Fitness in Rwanda**

**Version:** 2.0  
**Status:** Product Vision  
**Last Updated:** July 2026

---

# Mission

**Make Rwanda healthier through fitness.**

RwandaFitness exists to make fitness more accessible by connecting people with gyms, coaches, educational content, events, and a growing fitness community.

---

# Vision

RwandaFitness aims to become the leading fitness platform in Rwanda.

Our goal is not to manage gyms or replace their internal software. Instead, we are building the national digital ecosystem for fitness.

Anyone interested in fitness should think of RwandaFitness first.

---

# Product Positioning

RwandaFitness is:

- The national fitness discovery platform
- A trusted directory of gyms and coaches
- A fitness media platform
- A growing fitness community
- A future fitness marketplace

RwandaFitness is **not**:

- A gym management system
- An ERP
- A booking software only
- A simple directory

# Product Principles

Every new feature must answer **at least one** of these questions:

- Does it help people discover fitness?
- Does it help coaches?
- Does it help gyms?
- Does it educate users?
- Does it strengthen the community?
- Does it improve trust?

If the answer is **no**, the feature should not be built.
