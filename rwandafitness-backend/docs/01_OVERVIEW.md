# RwandaFitness — Overview

## Vision

RwandaFitness is a digital fitness platform built to connect people in Rwanda with coaches, gyms, fitness articles, and healthy lifestyle resources.

## Mission

To make fitness more accessible, organized, and visible across Rwanda by helping users discover trusted coaches, gyms, and practical fitness content.

## Target Users

- People looking for fitness coaches
- People looking for gyms
- Fitness coaches who want visibility
- Gym owners who want to promote their facilities
- Fitness enthusiasts looking for reliable articles and programs

## Core Features

- User registration and login
- Email verification
- Forgot password and reset password
- Coach profiles
- Gym profiles
- Coaching requests
- Articles and fitness content
- Image uploads
- Admin management through Django Admin

## Current Status

RwandaFitness is currently in MVP development. The main backend, frontend, authentication, articles, gyms, coaches, and email system are already implemented.
